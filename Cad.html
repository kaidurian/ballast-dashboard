Keys

Just start typing (anywhere) to enter a command — e.g. L, C, REC, A, PL, T, M, CO, TR, then Enter

Esc — cancel draw or Modify tool
Enter (with nothing typed) / dblclick — finish polyline; Enter also finishes a multi-Copy

Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y — undo/redo
